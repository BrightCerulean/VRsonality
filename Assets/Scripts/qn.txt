Q2 — PLAYGROUND (Age 5)
Question: "Another child is crying alone near the sandbox. What do you do?"

A. Go over and offer your toy             | Hero (H)      | Object: Toy Car
B. Sit next to them quietly               | Caregiver (C) | Object: Blanket
C. Watch from afar to understand why      | Sage (S)      | Object: Book
D. Go back to the swings — they'll be fine| Explorer (E)  | Object: Ball

- Toy Car  → near sandbox
- Blanket  → on bench
- Book     → under tree
- Ball     → near slide
